Who's Daemon Blackfyre

Daemon Blackfyre (The Black Dragon) is a fictional character first mentioned in the book A Storm of Swords (published in 2000) and later in the book Fire & Blood (published in 2018) by the famous author George R.R. Martin who wrote the A Song of Ice and Fire series his name appears in the ancient history of house targaryen and the Blackfyre rebellion
